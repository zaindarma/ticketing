News

Jakarta, 29 June 2024 - Live Nation, TEM Presents, and PK Entertainment announce that the unstoppable hitmaker and 14-time GRAMMY award-winning artist, Bruno Mars, will return to Indonesia on September 13th-14th 2024, where he will be performing at the Jakarta International Stadium. The announcement follows news of his monumental 14-show run in Brazil happening this October.

Fourteen-time GRAMMY Award winner and thirty-time GRAMMY Award nominee Bruno Mars is a celebrated singer, songwriter, producer, and musician who has sold over 200 million singles worldwide, making him one of the best-selling artists of all time.

Tickets for the 13 & 14 September shows are OFFICIALLY SOLD OUT. Limited tickets are still available for the 11 September show exclusively at brunomarsinjakarta.com. VIP packages are also available at vip.livenation.asia.

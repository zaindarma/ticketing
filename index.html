News

Jakarta - Thursday, 27 March 2025 – Sound Rhythm proudly presents the highly-anticipated debut performance of Japan’s renowned rock band, MY FIRST STORY, live for the first time ever in Indonesia. The concert is set to take place on Saturday, 26 July 2025 at Tennis Indoor Senayan, Jakarta.

MY FIRST STORY, a rock band from Shibuya, Tokyo, formed in 2011, quickly gained popularity for their dynamic music and energetic live performances. The band consists of Hiro (vocals), Teru (guitar), Nob (bass), and Kid'z (drums).
